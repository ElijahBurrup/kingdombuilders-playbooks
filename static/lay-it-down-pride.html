Before We Begin

"The Throne You Sit on Every Day"

Pride is not arrogance. Arrogance is just pride dressed loud. Real pride is the voice that says "I don't need help." The reflex that says "I already knew that." The identity built entirely on what you've accomplished, curated, and controlled.

Chapters 1–2

"The Curator" & "The Image Fast"

You stopped sharing and started curating. Every photo is angled. Every story is edited. Every conversation has a narrator running in the background. This pair identifies the altar of self-image, then gives you a 7-day protocol to dismantle it.

Chapters 3–4

"The Last Word" & "The Surrender Protocol"

Before someone finishes correcting you, the rebuttal is already loaded. This pair exposes the addiction to being right and gives you the Five-Second Rule, the Eighty Percent Practice, and the Weekly Ask to retrain your reflexes.

Chapter 5

"The Root Beneath the Root"

Pride keeps regenerating because it is protecting a wound. Somewhere you learned "nobody is coming." This bridge chapter traces the architecture of self-reliance back to its origin and rewires identity from performance to sonship.

Chapters 6–7

"The One-Man Army" & "The Dependency Drill"

"I'll just do it myself" became the most expensive sentence in your life. This pair names the altar of self-reliance and gives you the Ask Protocol, the Delegation Fast, and the Submission Practice to tear it down.

Chapter 8

"The Low Place"

Humility is not the floor. It is the foundation. The lower you go, the stronger the foundation. The stronger the foundation, the higher He can build. This closing chapter installs a Daily Posture practice for staying low without losing strength.
