Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase The Conductor's Playbook, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your purchase and send follow-up emails
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe (we never see or store your card details)
  • Download activity — IP address and timestamp when you download your file

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver your purchased product via email and download link
  • Send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, delivery)
  • Send follow-up educational emails (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Prevent fraud and enforce download limits

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your information to third parties
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any cookie-based tracking
  • We do not share your email with marketing partners

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — email delivery (Resend Privacy Policy)
  • Render — web hosting (Render Privacy Policy)

5. Data Retention

We retain purchase records and email logs for business and tax purposes. Download logs are retained for 90 days. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact support@kingdombuilders.ai.
