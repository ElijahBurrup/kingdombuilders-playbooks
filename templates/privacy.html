Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Kingdom Builders AI ("we," "us," "our") operates the website at kingdombuilders.ai and related services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

By using our website or purchasing our products, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name and email address when you create an account or make a purchase
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your full credit card number
  • Usage data: pages visited, playbooks read, reading progress, and interaction with features such as the discovery engine and reading paths
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, and screen size for optimizing your experience
  • Email engagement: whether emails we send are opened or clicked, to improve our communications

3. Google OAuth

If you sign in using Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We use this information solely to create and authenticate your account. We do not access your Google contacts, files, or any other Google services data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver purchased content and manage your account
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Send transactional emails (purchase confirmations, password resets)
  • Send educational follow up emails related to your purchases (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Track reading progress and award achievement stamps
  • Improve our products and user experience

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use the following cookies and browser storage:

  • Session cookies: to keep you logged in during your visit
  • Authentication tokens: secure JWT tokens to verify your identity
  • Preferences: local storage for UI preferences such as filter selections

We do not use advertising cookies or cross site tracking cookies.

6. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services to operate:

  • Stripe for payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Resend for transactional email delivery (Resend Privacy Policy)
  • Google for OAuth sign in (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics for understanding site usage and traffic patterns (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Render for web hosting (Render Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare for CDN and security (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any marketing partners or data brokers.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and purchase records for as long as your account is active and as required for business and tax purposes. Usage analytics are retained in aggregate form. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

8. Data Security

We use industry standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords (bcrypt), and secure token based authentication. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email support@kingdombuilders.ai.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@kingdombuilders.ai.
