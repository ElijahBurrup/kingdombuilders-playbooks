The Forest, Dusk

Darter found Quill eating bark. Fang was circling at the tree line.

"How are you not running?"

"Fang is not my problem. Fang is Fang's problem. The moment Fang touches me, Fang is reminded of a lesson Fang has already learned. I do not have to remember the lesson for it to work. The quills remember."

If someone calls after 9pm, your phone goes on Do Not Disturb at 8:58 automatically. You do not decide. The architecture decided. If someone repeatedly cancels, you stop making singular plans with them — you make plans that work whether they show up. The consequence is structural, not emotional. The lock does the work the guard used to do.
