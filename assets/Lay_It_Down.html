Chapter One

Comfortable Addictions

When God calls deeper, He asks you to lay something down.

Most Christians would never touch heroin. And yet millions cannot function without coffee, sugar, energy drinks, snacks. The issue is not enjoyment. The issue is reliance. When peace depends on it. When patience depends on it. When normal depends on it. At that point, the issue is authority.

If sugar were neutral, moderation would work. For many, moderation has already failed quietly. Smokers do not heal by having a few cigarettes weekly. Some substances cross a line where zero becomes the only honest starting point.
